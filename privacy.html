Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 2026

1. Introduction

Thank you for using Okinawa Survival Guide ("the App"). This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use and protect your information when you use our mobile application.

Okinawa Survival Guide is a Japanese language learning app designed for people living in or moving to Okinawa, Japan. We are committed to protecting your privacy.

2. Information We Collect

We collect NO personal information.

Okinawa Survival Guide does not require you to create an account, provide an email address, or share any personal details to use the app.

Data stored on your device only:

The app stores the following data LOCALLY on your device using your phone's storage:

  • Your learning progress (words learned, streaks, badges earned)
  • Your game scores and high scores
  • Your difficulty preferences and settings
  • Words marked as "learned" or "still learning"
  • Sound effects preferences (on/off)

This data never leaves your device. It is not sent to any server. It is not accessible to us or any third party.

3. Text-to-Speech (Audio)

The app uses your device's built-in Text-to-Speech (TTS) system to speak Japanese phrases aloud. This feature:

  • Uses your device's local TTS engine
  • Does not send audio data to external servers
  • Does not record your voice
  • Works completely offline

4. Internet Connection

Okinawa Survival Guide is designed to work fully offline after installation. The Emergency Phrases section works offline and does not require an internet connection.

The app does not make any network requests during normal use.

5. Third-Party Services

Google Play Store: If you downloaded the app through Google Play, Google's own privacy policy applies to the download and payment process. We receive no personal information from this transaction beyond what Google provides to all developers (aggregate download statistics).

In-App Purchases: The app uses Google Play's billing system for any future content purchases. We do not collect or store your payment information.

6. Children's Privacy

Okinawa Survival Guide does not knowingly collect any information from children under 13 years of age. The app contains no advertising, no social features, and no data collection of any kind, making it safe for all ages.

7. Data Security

Since all data is stored locally on your device and we collect no personal information, there is no data transmitted to secure. Your learning progress is as secure as your own device.

If you delete the app, all locally stored progress data will be deleted with it.

8. Your Rights

Since we collect no personal data, there is nothing to request, delete or export. Your data belongs entirely to you and exists only on your device.

9. Changes to This Policy

If we update this Privacy Policy we will update the "Last updated" date at the top of this page. Continued use of the app after any changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

For significant changes we will notify users through an app update notice.
