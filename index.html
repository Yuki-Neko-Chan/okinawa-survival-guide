Not Japan — Okinawa

Okinawa has its own dialect, culture and traditions. This app teaches you both.

はいさい！ Haisai! Hello in Okinawan dialect (male) — locals will love this
はいたい！ Haitai! Hello in Okinawan dialect (female) — instant smiles guaranteed
にふぇーでびる！ Nifee deebiru! Thank you in Okinawan — the phrase that opens every door
ぬちどぅ宝 Nuchi du takara Life is the greatest treasure — the famous Okinawan proverb
サーターアンダギー Saataa andagii Okinawan fried doughnuts — a must-try at every festival
エイサー Eisaa Traditional Okinawan drum dance — the heartbeat of the island
