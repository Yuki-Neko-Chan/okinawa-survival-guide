🌺 Okinawa-Specific Content

Not just Japan — Okinawa

Okinawa has its own culture, food, festivals, and history that set it apart. The app's Culture & Etiquette guide covers what makes the island unique — including a handful of key Okinawan phrases that locals genuinely appreciate hearing.

🌺
Culture & Etiquette
Bowing, gift giving, removing shoes, dining rules, onsen etiquette — everything you need to navigate daily life respectfully.
🥁
Festivals & Traditions
Eisaa drum dancing, Naha Tug-of-War, Shurijo Castle, Obon season — what to expect and how to participate.
🍜
Okinawan Food
Sōki soba, goya champuru, sātā andagī, Orion beer — learn the local food and drink culture so you can order with confidence.
🗣️
Key Okinawan Phrases
A few essential phrases locals love to hear — like はいさい (Haisai) and にふぇーでびる (Nifee deebiru). Not a dialect course, just the words that open doors.
🌀
Typhoon Season
What to buy, what the warnings mean, where the shelters are, and how to talk to neighbors and stores when a storm is coming.
🤝
Neighbor Relations
How to introduce yourself, exchange gifts, handle noise concerns, and build real connections with your Japanese neighbors.
